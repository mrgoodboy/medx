Our awesome members

Hatice Nur Eken

Co-President

Hatice Nur Eken is a junior in Jonathan Edwards College majoring in cognitive science. She is very excited to serve as the co-president of MedX next year. Hailing from Turkey, she has a passion for writing, traveling, trying different cuisines, playing the piano and learning languages. Nur is particularly interested in the relationship between law and medicine, especially in the realm of psychiatry and mental health. Apart from MedX, she is also a member of the Yale Political Union at Yale and enjoys their debates.

Kasidet Manakongtreecheep

Co-president

Kasidet Manakongtreecheep is a sophomore (class of 2017) at Yale University. Before coming to Yale, Kasidet spent his summer in Xiangya hospital in Hunan province, shadowing doctors and learning about the physician-patient relationship in China. At Yale, Kasidet is majoring in Molecular Biophysics and Biochemistry and a Global Health Fellow. Kasidet’s interests lies in health policy of developing countries, especially in Universal Healthcare system and physician-patient relationship. He is also the president of the Thai students’ association at Yale. Outside schoolwork, Kasidet enjoys spending time doing his lab research and watching Manchester United.

Anne Zlatow

Secretary

Anne Zlatow is a Silliman freshman pre-med majoring in East Asian Languages and Literatures. She is the current secretary for both MedX and Kasama: The Filipino Club at Yale. Anne grew up in Arizona, spending summers in the Philippines. Her interest in medicine stems from shadowing and internship experiences at Davao Doctors Hospital, University of Mindanao Multitest and Diagnostic Center, and Carondelet Imaging Center. Long-term, Anne aspires to be a radiologist fluent in Chinese.

Angie Fuentes

Treasurer

Angie Fuentes is a junior in Berkeley College from a suburb of Chicago, Illinois. She is majoring in Cognitive Science and hopes to attend medical school after graduation. Angie is interested in the brain specifically and the mechanisms it uses for decision we make. On campus, she is a member of the club ski team and works in a primate cognitive neuroscience lab. Angie is very excited to take on the role of Treasurer for MedX and looks forward to all that the group will have to offer this year!

Nicholas King

Director of Xamine (Education)

Nicholas King grew up in Boston, Massachusetts and as a young child travelled frequently with his family. He especially enjoys the personal aspect of medicine; he has had a great time volunteering in hospitals and working as an EMT and waiter. In the future he is looking forward to working in bioengineering research and is considering medical school. He is Jonathan Edwards Class of 2018.

Sophia Yin

Director of Xecute (Policy)

Sophia is a sophomore in Berkeley College at Yale. She studies psychology and is planning to go to medical school. Sophia is pursuing medicine because the honor of being able to help others at such a critical time of their lives is unmatched. In addition, the importance of effective healthcare is an issue that unites all people, crossing the lines of both time and place. In her free time, Sophia can often be found browsing Buzzfeed and singing (sometimes at the same time).
